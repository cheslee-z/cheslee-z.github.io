Chenlong Zhang

Jan 1, 0001

I’m a master student at BUAA advised by Prof. Dawei Li. I have a passion for robots (especially quadcopters), and I’m committed to exploring their possible future applications. Specifically, my current research interests mainly focus on the autonomous navigation of UAVs in dynamic environments, which involves the whole process of perception, localization, mapping, planning and control. Although this sounds like a lot of work, it’s okay, because I hope to become a full-stack robotic algorithm engineer in the future.

Alice Wu 吳恩達
